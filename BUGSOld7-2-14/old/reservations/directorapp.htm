If you are a current Brown or RISD student, please do not be hesitant to apply, even if you have never directed before. We welcome all applications and consider ourselves a group whose purpose includes fun and experimentation in addition to putting on quality Gilbert and Sullivan productions. Please note that due to some technical difficulties, any applications submitted before 5pm on Sunday 5/3 were not received. If you submitted a director's app before that time, please resubmit! I (the webmaster) am sincerely sorry about this confusion.

To apply to direct the spring show of Iolanthe, either

  1. return this form to Eva Kurtz-Nelson by 11:59PM on Sunday May 3rd.
  2. fill out the form below by 11:59PM on Sunday May 3rd.
    • Do not leave any parts of the form blank. If you do not have a response to a form field, just enter "None."

Interviews with the board will be on the afternoon of Monday May 4th. If you have conflicts or questions, email bugs@brown.edu.

please note, late applications will not be accepted

Describe any other experiences (including musical ones) you think may be relevant to the board's decision and to your directorial experience.
List all expected commitments for next semester (club meetings, jobs, class, lab, etc.).
Explain what you envision your task as director to be. Be sure to include how you will find a production staff and how you will work with them.

In a 1-2 page (300-600 words) statement, explain your proposal. Try to answer the following questions: why does this show excite you?; what is your underlying creative vision for the production (feel free to reference other performances you may have seen)?; and why are you the best person for the job?
