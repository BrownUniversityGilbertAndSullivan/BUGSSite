Have you always dreamed of being a British aristocrat? A fairy? Pirate? Princess? Do you have decades of theater experience that you’re just dying to share, or are you getting interested in musical theater for the very first time?

If any of the above is true of you, and even if it isn’t, we want you to join us!

It has been said (see here) that BUGS is “one of the most welcoming theater groups you are likely to find” so don’t fear! Even if you have no previous theater experience, there’s a place for you somewhere in our productions.

Every semester we produce a fully-staged and orchestrated show, involving actors, singers, and dancers; directors, assistant directors, music directors, and conductors; orchestra musicians and rehearsal pianists; set, costume, props, lighting, and makeup designers and artists; tech crews, stage managers, and production managers; videographers, ushers, and a host of other volunteers. The only thing you can’t be if you’ve never worked with us before is a board member.

Brown undergraduates, grad students, and community members are all welcome to be in our productions. We need everyone’s skills and energy! Our casts are formed of theater majors and amateurs, those who have sung for 14 years and those who have never been on stage before.
