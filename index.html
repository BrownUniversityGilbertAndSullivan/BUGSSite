Congratulations to the cast of Brown University Gilbert and Sullivan's spring musical, A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to the Forum!

Inspired by the farces of ancient Roman playwright Plautus, this musical tells the story of the Roman slave Pseudolus' attempts to win freedom by helping young Hero woo the girl next door. Full of puns, satire, and lots of humor, this show is perfect for both new and seasoned audiences alike!

Music and lyrics by Stephen Sondheim
Book by Burt Shevelove '37 and Larry Gelbart
Directed by Paul Martino '17
Music directed by Jeff Ball '17
Assistant directed by Ani Mack '17
